Our Roots

We started Off The Hook Poke Market in 2018 with one goal in mind: to serve the freshest and best Poke in Hawaii. Thanks to our hard working and dedicated crew, in 2019 we won the Ilima Award for Hawaii's Best Seafood Restaurant. We continue to grow and learn in our ongoing quest for excellence.
